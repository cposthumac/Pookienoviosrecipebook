Butter Chicken

This rich and flavorful Butter Chicken is cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce with warm spices, perfect for pairing with rice or naan.

Ingredients:

For the Marinade:

  • 3/4 cup Full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin (optional; or more garam masala)
  • 1 tsp Sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp Ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  • 2 lb Boneless skinless chicken thighs

For the Butter Chicken Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 large Onion (sliced thinly)
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 29-oz can Tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin (optional; or more garam masala)
  • 1 tsp Ground ginger
  • 1 tsp Chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp Sea salt (to taste)
  • Cilantro (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, stir together all the marinade ingredients. Add the chicken thighs, stir to coat, and cover with plastic wrap. Chill for at least 1 hour, or overnight.
  2. Remove the chicken from the marinade and use kitchen shears to cut into bite-sized pieces. No need to wipe the marinade off the chicken, but leave behind what isn’t stuck to it.
  3. Melt the butter in a large sauté pan over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and sauté for 8-10 minutes, until soft and browned.
  4. Make a well in the center and add the minced garlic. Sauté for about 30 seconds, until fragrant, then stir in with the onions.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, heavy cream, garam masala, cumin, ground ginger, chili powder, and sea salt. Adjust salt to taste; other flavors will develop as it cooks.
  6. Add the chicken pieces to the pan, nestling them in the sauce in a single layer. Spoon additional sauce over the chicken to cover.
  7. Increase heat to high and bring to a simmer, then simmer for 8-10 minutes, stirring every few minutes, until the sauce is slightly thickened and the chicken is cooked through. Adjust salt to taste if needed.
  8. Garnish with cilantro (optional) and serve hot with rice or naan.
