Spinach & Feta Eggs

Active Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 10 mins | Servings: 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add spinach and saut√© until wilted.
  3. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set.
  4. Sprinkle with feta and serve immediately.
