Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish with tender chicken pieces and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro and mint for garnish
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fried onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook basmati rice with water and a pinch of salt, set aside.
