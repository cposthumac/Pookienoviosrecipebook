Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish with tender chicken pieces and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro and mint for garnish
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fried onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook basmati rice with water and a pinch of salt, set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds and bay leaf, then saut√© onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, chili powder, garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Mix in the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through.
  6. Add yogurt to the chicken mixture and cook for 5-10 minutes on low heat.
  7. Layer the cooked rice on top of the chicken mixture, then cover and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro, mint, and fried onions (optional).
  9. Serve hot with raita or a side of salad.
