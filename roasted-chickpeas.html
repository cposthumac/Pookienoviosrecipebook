Roasted Chickpeas

Prep Time: 5 min | Total Time: 45 min | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon Splenda brown sugar blend
