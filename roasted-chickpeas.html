Roasted and Spiced Chickpeas

This high-fiber snack is a much healthier choice than chips and is a great alternative for those with nut allergies. Perfect for snacking or as a crunchy topping for soups and salads.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz.) garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil (divided)
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • ½ tsp. cumin
  • ½ tsp. chili powder
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. Splenda brown sugar blend

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix garbanzo beans with 1 Tbsp. olive oil, cinnamon, cumin, chili powder, and salt.
  3. Spread the bean mixture evenly on the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 40–45 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes, until beans are crispy and dry.
  4. Remove from the oven and transfer hot beans to a medium bowl. Add 1 Tbsp. olive oil and Splenda brown sugar blend. Mix well.
  5. Spread beans on parchment paper and allow to cool for 20 minutes before serving.

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size: ¼ cup | Servings: 6

  • Calories: 120
  • Total Fat: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 16g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Protein: 4g
