Almond Butter & Roasted Grape Toast

A simple, sweet, and nutty toast recipe for a quick breakfast or snack!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup seedless red grapes
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 slice whole-grain bread, toasted

Directions

Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Place grapes on a baking sheet and roast until skins are crisp but grapes remain soft inside, about 15 minutes. Spread almond butter on toast and top with roasted grapes.
