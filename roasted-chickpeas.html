Roasted and Spiced Chickpeas

This high-fiber snack is a much healthier choice than chips and is a great alternative for those with nut allergies. Perfect for parties or as a topping for soups and salads!

Ingredients

  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon Splenda brown sugar blend

Directions

Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray. In a medium bowl, mix garbanzo beans, olive oil, cinnamon, cumin, chili powder, and salt. Spread evenly on a baking sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes. Once crispy, toss with the brown sugar blend and cool for 20 minutes.

Nutrition Information

Serving size: 1/4 cup | Calories: 150 | Fat: 5g | Protein: 6g | Carbohydrates: 20g
