Massaman Curry

This rich and flavorful curry features tender beef or chicken in a creamy coconut-based sauce with spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and tamarind.

Ingredients:

For the Curry:

  • 1.5 lbs beef chuck, cut into 2" cubes (optional)
  • 2.5 cup coconut milk
  • 4–6 tsp curry paste
  • 2–3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 3 tbsp palm sugar, chopped
  • 2–3 tbsp tamarind juice
  • 2 medium potatoes (or 1 if using beef)
  • 1/2 large onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • Optional: green chilies (for extra heat)

For the Curry Paste:

  • 50 grams
