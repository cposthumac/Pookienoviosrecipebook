Salmon Bites

Salmon Bites are a crispy, flavorful appetizer made with fresh salmon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, for zest and juice
  • Toothpicks for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut salmon into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Mix olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Toss salmon cubes in the mixture to coat.
  5. Place salmon bites on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Remove from oven, squeeze lemon juice on top, and garnish with parsley.
  8. Serve with toothpicks for easy picking!
