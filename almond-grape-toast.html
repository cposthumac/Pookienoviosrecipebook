Almond Grape Toast

Active Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 10 mins | Servings: 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1/4 cup red grapes, halved
  • Honey (optional)

Directions:

  1. Toast the bread to your desired crispiness.
  2. Spread almond butter on top of the toast.
  3. Top with halved grapes and drizzle with honey if desired.
  4. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Information:

  • Calories: 220
  • Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 1g
  • Cholesterol: 0mg
  • Carbohydrates: 26g
  • Protein: 5g
  • Fiber: 4g
