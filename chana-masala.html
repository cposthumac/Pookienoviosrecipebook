Chana Masala

Chana Masala is a popular Indian dish made with chickpeas and a blend of flavorful spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas (canned or dried)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur powder (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin and let it splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste, cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, green chili, and cook until tomatoes soften.
  5. Add dry spices and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Add chickpeas and water to make a thick gravy. Cook for 15 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot!
