Spinach & Egg Toast

Active Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 10 mins | Servings: 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Toast the bread to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add spinach and saut√© until wilted.
  3. Crack the egg into the skillet and cook to your preferred doneness. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the cooked egg on top of the toast and serve hot.
