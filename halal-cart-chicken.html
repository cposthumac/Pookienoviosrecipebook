Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Ground black pepper
  • Kosher salt

For the Rice

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 2.5 cups chicken stock (or water)

For the White Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 tbsp sugar (or up to 1 tbsp)
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp ground caraway
  • Sprinkle of turmeric (for color)
  • 1/4 tsp ground sumac (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lots of black pepper
  • Salt to taste

To Serve

  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 1 tomato, cut into wedges

Instructions

Combine lemon juice, oregano, coriander, garlic, and olive oil in a blender; blend until smooth. Place chicken in a shallow bowl, add half the marinade and stir to coat. Cover and put chicken in fridge for 1–4 hours, stirring occasionally. When ready, remove chicken and pat dry with paper towels; season with kosher salt and lots of pepper.

Heat a cast iron skillet over medium-high, adding some oil until lightly smoking. Add chicken and cook without disturbing until lightly browned on one side, about four minutes. Flip, reduce heat to medium, and cook until pieces reach 165º, about 6 minutes. Transfer to a cutting board and cool for 5 minutes. After resting, roughly chop into 1/4–1/2" pieces; add to a bowl and stir in remaining marinade.

Make the Rice

Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat, add turmeric and cumin until fragrant. Add rice and stir to coat; cook, stirring frequently, until lightly toasted, about 4 minutes. Transfer to rice cooker, add broth and cook until done.

Make the White Sauce

Combine all ingredients in a bowl, season with salt to taste.

To Serve

Heat chicken, with juices, in a skillet until heated through. Serve with rice, lettuce, tomato, pita, white sauce, and hot sauce of choice.
