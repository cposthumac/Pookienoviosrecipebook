Halal Cart Chicken

This recipe features marinated chicken served with fragrant rice, crisp vegetables, and a tangy white sauce. Itâ€™s the perfect meal for a delicious, filling dinner.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 lb Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Ground cumin
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp Ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Ground ginger
  • 1 tsp Sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper

For the White Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup Plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp White vinegar
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Dried dill
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper

For the Rice and Vegetables:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 3 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 Onion (sliced thinly)
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber (sliced thinly)
  • 1/2 cup Tomato (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together all the marinade ingredients. Add the chicken thighs, making sure they are evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. To make the white sauce, combine all the sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk together until smooth. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. In a saucepan, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Add the basmati rice and cook according to the package instructions. Once cooked, set aside and fluff with a fork.
